Mole Park: Whack & Win
Designed by the legendary Rex Flanagan, this party game is sure to delight, or inspire fright - because of how good it is, or incite... some celebrations! It's quite a light...hearted game about whacking moles until they're out of sight. Be Polite, invite your friends to play tonight! Just don't start a fight, despite how much you want to win!

Description

“Mole Park: Whack and Win!” invites you into the thrilling world of Molehaven, where becoming the ruler means mastering the art of Whacking moles and Winning prizes. Race to earn King Mole's crown and claim your rightful place as the monarch of Mole Park. Just remember: you have to Whack before you Win!

Game Overview

Mole Park progresses in Rounds (players do not take turns). Every round, you secretly choose whether you want to Whack or Win, and everyone rolls their dice at once:

  • To Whack, roll a Whacking die. If you roll a number, you get moles from that hole.
  • To Win, roll your Prize die. Cash in all your moles for a prize, including any stars from your prize dice.

Winning prizes will help you win better prizes the next time you cash in moles. Win the game by being the first player to win the Grand Prize!

Let’s meet our Moles!

Each mole is worth a certain number of stars, though not all 3-star moles will be the same! The mole deck will comprise 100 unique, professionally-illustrated moles, each with its own character that will delight everyone who whacks them!

Did someone say “Prizes”?

In Mole Park, you’ll find a colorful array of fabulous prize vendors which will accept your moles! Each prize vendor has an assortment of 10 prizes which you can win: 8 standard prizes and 2 exclusive prizes with powerful effects! But don’t worry if this sounds complicated, since only one prize from each vendor will be available at any given time.

Prizes and their corresponding star requirements:

  • 10 stars: Candy Shop
  • 15 stars: Toy emporium
  • 20 stars: Inflation Nation
  • 25 stars: Warm and Fuzzy
  • 30 stars: Sportstar!
  • 35 stars: Exclusive accessories
  • 40 stars: King Mole’s crown
