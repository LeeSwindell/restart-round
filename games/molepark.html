Mole Park
Whack & Win


Description

“Mole Park: Whack and Win!” invites you into the thrilling world of Molehaven, where becoming the ruler means mastering the art of Whacking moles and Winning prizes. Race to earn King Mole's crown and claim your rightful place as the monarch of Mole Park. Just remember: you have to Whack before you Win!

Game Overview

Mole Park progresses in Rounds (you do not take turns). Every round, you secretly choose whether you want to Whack or Win, and everyone rolls their dice at once:

  • To Whack, roll a Whacking die. If you roll a number, you get moles from that hole.
  • To Win, roll your Prize die. Cash in all your moles for a prize.

Winning prizes will help you win better prizes the next time you cash in moles. Win the game by being the first player to win the Grand Prize!

Let’s meet our Moles!

Each mole is worth a certain number of stars, though not all 3-star moles will be the same! The mole deck will comprise 100 unique, professionally-illustrated moles, each with its own character that will delight everyone who whacks them!

Did someone say “Prizes”?

Check 'em out!

In Mole Park, you’ll find a colorful array of fabulous prize vendors which will accept your moles! Each prize vendor has an assortment of prizes, though only one will be available each round!
