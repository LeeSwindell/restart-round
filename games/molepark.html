Mole Park: Whack 'n' Win
Designed by the legendary Rex Flanagan, this party game is sure to delight, or inspire fright - because of how good it is, or incite... some celebrations! It's quite a light...hearted game about whacking moles until they're out of sight. Be Polite, invite your friends to play tonight! Just don't start a fight, despite how much you want to win!
